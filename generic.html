BARCELONA

Image of the European modern art museum

¡Hola mundo!

Wellcome to Barcelona, one of the most emblematic cities in terms of Art in the world. By simply walking through its streets such as in "el Born", "el Raval", "Poblesec" or even a neighborhood like "Gracia", you can smell the art in every corner.

To get off to a good start, we highlight the museums that we consider most special in this beautiful city. We want you to know where to look when your idea is to flourish your creativity and culture with a touch of art.

As Pablo Picasso says "Everything you can imagina is real", and as I say, everithing you can find can be near you, on the best pieces of art here in Barcelona.
